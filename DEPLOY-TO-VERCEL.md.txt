# Deploy Critical Care Hub POC to Vercel

## 1) Sign in
Go to https://vercel.com and sign in (GitHub/GitLab/email).

## 2) Upload the folder
Zip the `critical-care-hub-poc` folder.
Click **New Project → Import → From your computer** and upload the zip.

## 3) Deploy
Use default settings → **Deploy**.
You’ll get a live link in ~20 seconds.

## Optional: Install as an app (PWA)
Open the link on your phone → “Add to Home Screen”.
Works offline for cached pages.
